Rules of the System

  • One cosmic signature only: a hairline constellation + celestial coordinates. Nothing else glows.
  • One accent (Oxide), three uses maximum per view — always marking a point of attention, never decoration.
  • Motion: a single 600ms rise-and-settle on the headline lines at load. Everything else is still.
  • Italics (Instrument Serif) are reserved for emphasis inside headlines — the voice, not a style.
  • 12-column grid, 64px margins, hairline rules carry the structure. Low density is the point.
